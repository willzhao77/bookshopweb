Book Repair and Restoration

Our restoration work encompasses books from the fifteenth century onwards. Much of the work we undertake includes family heirlooms: Bibles, dictionaries, Shakespeares, children's books and literature from the nineteenth and twentieth centuries. In addition, we advise procedures for the care of collections, and we undertake repair and restoration work on volumes in private libraries.
